Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. You can request the deletion of your Zuvora account and all associated data.

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1 — From the app:

  • Open FaceJam by Zuvora
  • Go to Settings (gear icon)
  • Tap Delete Account
  • Confirm the deletion

Option 2 — Via email:

Send an email to hello@zuvora.app with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 7 business days.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your profile and account information
  • All generated face swap images
  • Coin balances and transaction history
  • Usage and streak data

What to Know

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
  • If you have an active subscription, please cancel it through Google Play before deleting your account to avoid future charges.
  • Some anonymized, aggregated data may be retained for analytics purposes as described in our Privacy Policy.
